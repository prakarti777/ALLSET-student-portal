Government Exams After 12th (Arts Stream)

UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS)

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts India’s most prestigious exam for careers in the civil services. Arts students can appear after completing graduation in any stream. With subjects like History, Political Science, and Sociology, Arts students have an edge in optional papers. Successful candidates become IAS, IPS, IFS, or IRS officers and work at the highest levels of administration.

SSC CGL & CHSL

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts Combined Graduate Level (CGL) and Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) exams. These open opportunities in government ministries, income tax, customs, railways, and clerical services. Arts students can easily attempt these exams after 12th (for CHSL) and after graduation (for CGL).

State PCS (Provincial Civil Services)

State Public Service Commissions conduct PCS exams for administrative roles at the state level. Arts graduates with a background in subjects like History, Political Science, and Geography find these exams highly suitable. Successful candidates work as SDMs, Tehsildars, and State-level officers.

Banking Exams (IBPS/ SBI PO & Clerk)

Banking exams like IBPS PO, Clerk, and SBI PO are popular among Arts students after graduation. These jobs provide stability, career growth, and attractive salary packages. The exams test reasoning, general awareness, and English – areas where Arts students can perform well.

Defence Exams (NDA & CDS)

The National Defence Academy (NDA) and Combined Defence Services (CDS) exams are gateways to join the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force. Arts students can appear for NDA after 12th or CDS after graduation. These exams offer opportunities to serve the nation with pride and honor.

Railway Exams (RRB NTPC, Group D)

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) conducts exams for various posts in Indian Railways. Arts students can apply for NTPC, Group D, and clerical posts. Railways provide one of the largest employment opportunities in the public sector with job security and allowances.
