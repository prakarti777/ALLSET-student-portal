Jobs After BBA

Popular roles, average salaries in India (2025), and growth fields.

Management Trainee / Business Analyst

Role: Assisting managers, analyzing business operations, and preparing reports.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

Traditional management field; stable career path.

Marketing Executive / Digital Marketing Specialist

Role: Creating campaigns, managing social media, SEO, content marketing.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

Digital marketing with analytics skills is a new high-paying trend.

HR Executive / HR Manager

Role: Recruitment, employee engagement, payroll, training.

Average Salary: ₹3–8 LPA

HR analytics roles are high-paying and in demand.

Financial Analyst / Investment Analyst

Role: Budgeting, financial planning, investment evaluation, stock analysis.

Average Salary: ₹4–10 LPA

Corporate finance and fintech roles are high-paying.

Account Executive / Accountant

Role: Managing accounts, bookkeeping, taxation, audits.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

Specialized accounting like IFRS or GST consulting can pay higher.

Operations Manager / Supply Chain Executive

Role: Overseeing production, logistics, procurement, and process optimization.

Average Salary: ₹5–12 LPA

E-commerce and supply chain analytics are high-paying fields.

Business Development Executive / Sales Manager

Role: Generating leads, acquiring clients, improving revenue.

Average Salary: ₹4–10 LPA

Tech sales and SaaS roles are lucrative.

Entrepreneur / Startup Founder

Role: Running your own business or startup.

Salary: Variable, can be very high depending on success.

Startups in fintech, edtech, e-commerce are high-paying.

Management Consultant / Strategy Consultant

Role: Advising companies on business strategy, operations, and growth.

Average Salary: ₹8–25 LPA

Consulting for tech, finance, or international clients is high-paying.

Data Analyst / Business Intelligence Analyst

Role: Collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to aid business decisions.

Average Salary: ₹4–12 LPA

Data analytics is in high demand in e-commerce, fintech, and consulting.
