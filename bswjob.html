Jobs After BSW

Professional roles, average salaries in India (2025), and scope for social work graduates.

Social Worker (Community Development)

Role: Work with NGOs, government agencies, and welfare programs to improve health, education, and livelihoods.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

High demand in rural development and CSR projects of corporates.

Healthcare Social Worker

Role: Provide counseling and support to patients, coordinate rehabilitation programs in hospitals and clinics.

Average Salary: ₹3.5–7 LPA

Scope in public health, mental health programs, and government health missions.

Child Protection Officer

Role: Work with government departments and NGOs to safeguard children’s rights and welfare.

Average Salary: ₹4–8 LPA

BSW graduates can join UNICEF, Save the Children, and state child welfare boards.

Rehabilitation Counselor

Role: Assist individuals with disabilities, addiction recovery, or trauma to integrate into society.

Average Salary: ₹3–6.5 LPA

Opportunities in de-addiction centers, prisons, and rehabilitation homes.

Policy & Program Coordinator

Role: Implement and evaluate social welfare schemes at the district, state, or national level.

Average Salary: ₹5–10 LPA

Preferred by government projects, CSR foundations, and development consultancies.

Human Resource (HR) Executive – Social Sector

Role: Manage recruitment, employee welfare, and training in NGOs and social enterprises.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

BSW students with management training are valued in the development sector.
