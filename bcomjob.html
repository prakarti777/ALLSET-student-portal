Jobs After B.Com

Popular roles, average salaries in India (2025), and growth fields.

Accountant / Accounting Executive

Role: Bookkeeping, financial statements, GST compliance, and taxation.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

Additional certifications like Tally or IFRS can increase salary.

Financial Analyst

Role: Budgeting, financial planning, investment evaluation.

Average Salary: ₹4–8 LPA

Strong growth potential in finance & banking sectors.

Banking & Insurance Executive

Role: Working in banks, NBFCs, insurance companies for operations or customer management.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

PSU banks are stable; fintech banking roles are high-paying.

Tax Consultant / GST Consultant

Role: Helping businesses with tax filing, GST compliance, and audits.

Average Salary: ₹4–10 LPA

Taxation is a high-demand skill; certification boosts salary.

Accounts Assistant / Junior Auditor

Role: Assisting senior auditors in audits and financial reviews.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

Pathway to CA, CMA, or CS professional careers.

Business Development Executive

Role: Generating leads, managing clients, improving revenue streams.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

Sales and business development are lucrative in startups and IT firms.
