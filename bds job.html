Jobs & Careers After BDS

Discover government jobs, higher studies, private practice, and global opportunities available after completing BDS.

Government Jobs & Exams

UPSC CMS (Dental)

Opportunities through Combined Medical Services exam for dental officers.

State PSC Recruitments

Government dental surgeon posts through state public service commissions.

Army Dental Corps

Serve in the Armed Forces as a commissioned dental officer.

Higher Studies & Specializations

MDS

Master of Dental Surgery with specialization options like Orthodontics, Prosthodontics, etc.

PG Diplomas

Short-term programs in clinical dentistry, implantology, and cosmetic dentistry.

Fellowships

Specialized fellowships in endodontics, oral surgery, and dental aesthetics.

Clinical & Private Practice

Own Dental Clinic

Start your own dental clinic and build independent practice.

Associate Dentist

Work as an associate in private hospitals, dental chains, or clinics.

Corporate Hospitals

Opportunities in large private hospitals and healthcare companies.

Research & International Careers

Teaching & Research

Join dental colleges as faculty or pursue PhD in dental sciences.

NBDE (USA)

National Board Dental Examination for practicing dentistry in the USA.

ADC (Australia)

Australian Dental Council exam for dental practice in Australia.

Other Abroad Options

Licensing exams for Canada, UK, and Middle East for global careers.

Other Career Options

Dental Cosmetology

Careers in smile design, aesthetic and cosmetic dentistry.

Forensic Odontology

Specialized field applying dental science in crime investigation.

Public Health

Work in government and NGOs for oral healthcare awareness and programs.

Dental Writing

Content writing, research publication, and academic writing in dentistry.
