Jobs After B.Com (Hons.)

Popular roles, average salaries in India (2025), and growth fields.

Chartered Accountant (CA) / Finance Executive

Role: Accounting, auditing, taxation, and financial advisory.

Average Salary: ₹5–15 LPA

B.Com (Hons.) provides a stronger base for professional finance careers.

Company Secretary (CS) / Legal Compliance Officer

Role: Corporate law compliance, company governance, secretarial work.

Average Salary: ₹6–12 LPA

High-paying corporate jobs, requires CS professional certification.

Cost & Management Accountant (CMA)

Role: Cost analysis, budgeting, management accounting.

Average Salary: ₹5–12 LPA

High-demand field in manufacturing and service industries.

Financial Analyst / Investment Banker

Role: Investment planning, stock market analysis, corporate finance.

Average Salary: ₹6–20 LPA

Hons. students have an edge in analytical and finance roles.

Auditor / Tax Consultant

Role: Corporate audits, GST and tax consultancy, compliance.

Average Salary: ₹4–10 LPA

Specialized taxation knowledge adds value; high-paying in consulting firms.

Business Analyst / Management Consultant

Role: Strategy, operations, and data-driven business recommendations.

Average Salary: ₹8–20 LPA

Consulting is a growing, high-paying field for Hons. graduates.

Banking / Fintech Executive

Role: Corporate banking, investment operations, digital finance.

Average Salary: ₹5–15 LPA

Fintech sector is booming; Hons. graduates are preferred for analytical roles.
