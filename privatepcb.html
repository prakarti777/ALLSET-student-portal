Private Sector After PCB

PCB students can also find great career opportunities in private hospitals, laboratories, pharmaceutical industries, and biotech companies.

Private Hospitals

Work as doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, or lab technicians in reputed hospitals.

Pharmaceutical Companies

Jobs in drug manufacturing, sales, quality control, and R&D departments.

Research Labs

Opportunities in biotechnology, microbiology, and genetics labs for research roles.

Biotech Firms

Work in genetic engineering, agriculture biotech, and food technology companies.

Healthcare Management

Roles in hospital administration, medical coding, and health data management.
