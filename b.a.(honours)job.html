Jobs After B.A. (Honours)

Specialized roles, average salaries in India (2025), and growth opportunities.

Research Scholar / Academic Career

Role: Pursue M.A., M.Phil, or Ph.D. for research and teaching in specialized honours subjects.

Average Salary: ₹4–10 LPA (as lecturer/researcher)

UGC NET/JRF clears the path to permanent faculty positions.

Policy Analyst / Political Consultant

Role: Analyze government policies, work with think tanks, NGOs, and international organizations.

Average Salary: ₹5–12 LPA

Popular for Political Science and Economics Honours graduates.

Economist / Data Analyst

Role: Market research, economic forecasting, and financial data analysis.

Average Salary: ₹6–15 LPA

Economics Honours is highly valued in finance, banking, and government institutions.

Corporate Communication & Media Roles

Role: PR executive, corporate communication specialist, or media strategist.

Average Salary: ₹4–9 LPA

English and Sociology Honours students excel in creative and communication-heavy industries.

Civil Services / Competitive Exams

Role: Administrative services (IAS, PCS, SSC, UPSC exams).

Average Salary: ₹7–18 LPA (varies by position)

B.A. Honours provides deeper subject mastery for UPSC/State PCS exams.

Content Writer / Subject Expert

Role: Writing, editing, and creating academic, research-based, or professional content.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

High scope in publishing houses, ed-tech companies, and corporate knowledge roles.
