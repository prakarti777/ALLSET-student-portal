Jobs After B.A. (General)

Popular roles, average salaries in India (2025), and growth fields.

Teacher / Lecturer (School & College)

Role: Teaching in schools (after B.Ed) or colleges (after NET/PhD).

Average Salary: ₹3–8 LPA

High demand in government & private institutions; UGC NET boosts opportunities.

Civil Services / Government Jobs

Role: Administrative services (IAS, PCS, SSC, UPSC exams).

Average Salary: ₹6–15 LPA (varies by role)

Most B.A. graduates opt for competitive exams; Arts subjects align well with UPSC/SSC/PCS.

Journalist / Media Executive

Role: Reporting, content writing, editing, digital media management.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

English, Political Science, and Mass Communication students have strong scope in this field.

Social Worker / NGO Executive

Role: Working with NGOs, welfare organizations, and government projects.

Average Salary: ₹2.5–5 LPA

Growth in public policy, CSR, and development studies careers.

Law & Legal Services

Role: After BA LLB or LLB (post-B.A.), working as advocate, legal advisor, or corporate lawyer.

Average Salary: ₹4–12 LPA

Many B.A. graduates pursue law; Arts subjects help in CLAT and judiciary exams.

Content Writer / Editor

Role: Writing, editing, and managing creative and professional content.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

Strong demand in digital marketing, publishing houses, and corporate sectors.
