Jobs & Careers After MBBS

Explore government exams, higher studies, hospital careers, and global opportunities available after completing MBBS.

Government Jobs & Exams

UPSC CMS

Combined Medical Services exam for government medical officer posts.

AIIMS/State PSC

Recruitment exams for doctors in AIIMS and various state services.

Armed Forces Medical Services

Join the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force as a commissioned medical officer.

Higher Studies & Specializations

MD/MS

Postgraduate medical degrees in various specializations after MBBS.

DNB

Diplomate of National Board program offering specialist training.

PG Diplomas

Short-term specialized diploma programs in medical fields.

Clinical & Hospital Careers

Resident Doctor

Work as junior resident or senior resident in hospitals and medical colleges.

Private Practice

Set up independent clinics or join private hospitals.

Hospital Administration

Management and administration roles in healthcare institutions.

Research & International Opportunities

WHO & NGOs

Opportunities in international health organizations and NGOs.

USMLE

United States licensing exam for practicing medicine in the USA.

PLAB

Professional and Linguistic Assessments Board exam for UK medical practice.

FMGE

Foreign Medical Graduate Exam for Indian citizens with MBBS abroad.

Other Options

Medical Writing

Careers in healthcare communication, publishing, and content creation.

Public Health

Work in preventive medicine, epidemiology, and healthcare policy.

HealthTech Startups

Opportunities in digital health, telemedicine, and healthcare innovation.
