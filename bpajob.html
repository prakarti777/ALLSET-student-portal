Jobs After BPA (Bachelor of Performing Arts)

Performance-based roles, average salaries in India (2025), and career scope.

Performer (Music/Dance/Drama)

Role: Work as a professional singer, dancer, musician, or theatre actor.

Average Salary: ₹3–8 LPA (varies with performance opportunities)

Live concerts, theatre productions, TV reality shows, and OTT platforms are major career avenues.

Music / Dance Teacher

Role: Teaching classical or contemporary music and dance in schools, academies, or as private tutors.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

High demand in schools, cultural centers, and online learning platforms.

Theatre / Drama Artist

Role: Acting in plays, street theatre, films, and TV serials.

Average Salary: ₹4–9 LPA

Scope in stage productions, Bollywood, OTT, and independent theatre groups.

Choreographer

Role: Create and direct dance sequences for films, TV, and stage shows.

Average Salary: ₹5–12 LPA

Popular in Bollywood, music videos, and large-scale stage productions.

Music Composer / Director

Role: Compose and produce music for albums, films, or theatre.

Average Salary: ₹6–15 LPA (varies widely)

Independent music industry, OTT shows, and advertising offer strong opportunities.

Cultural Program Coordinator

Role: Organize cultural events, festivals, and performing arts workshops.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

NGOs, cultural trusts, and government bodies hire performing arts graduates for event management.
