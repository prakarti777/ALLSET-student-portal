Jobs After BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts)

Creative career options, average salaries in India (2025), and industry scope.

Graphic Designer

Role: Design logos, branding, UI/UX, and digital art for companies and agencies.

Average Salary: ₹3–6 LPA

High demand in IT, advertising, digital marketing, and startups.

Animator / Multimedia Artist

Role: Create animations, VFX, 3D art, and multimedia content for films and games.

Average Salary: ₹4–8 LPA

Popular in Bollywood, gaming studios, OTT platforms, and VFX companies.

Illustrator / Creative Artist

Role: Work on book illustrations, comics, advertising visuals, and freelance art.

Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA

Growing demand in publishing, advertising, and children’s content.

Art Director

Role: Lead creative teams in advertising, films, and design studios.

Average Salary: ₹6–12 LPA

Requires experience and portfolio; top role in creative industries.

Fine Artist / Painter

Role: Independent artist, exhibitions, commissioned works, and galleries.

Average Salary: ₹2–8 LPA (can grow higher with recognition)

Scope for national/international exhibitions and private collections.

Art Teacher / Professor

Role: Teach fine arts in schools, colleges, or private academies.

Average Salary: ₹4–9 LPA

MFA/Ph.D. is required for higher education teaching roles.
