Government Exams

SSC CHSL

For clerical posts in central government offices.

NDA

National Defence Academy entry.offers entry into the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Railway Recruitment (RRB)

Clerical and assistant-level posts in Indian Railways.

Professional Courses Entrance Exams

CA Foundation

Chartered Accountancy exam conducted by ICAI.

CMA Foundation

Cost & Management Accountancy exam conducted by ICAI (Cost).

CS Foundation

Company Secretary exam conducted by ICSI.

CFA

International Chartered Financial Analyst exam (after graduation, prep can start early).

Management & Business Entrance Exams

CUET UG

Common University Entrance Test for B.Com, BBA, Economics, etc.

IPMAT

Integrated Program in Management at IIM Indore & Rohtak.

NPAT

Narsee Monjee’s exam for BBA, B.Com, B.Sc Finance.

SET

Symbiosis Entrance Test for BBA, B.Com, Economics.

Christ University Test

For admission into BBA, B.Com, Economics courses.

Finance, Economics & Law Exams

CLAT

Common Law Admission Test for BA LLB, BBA LLB.

AILET

Law entrance exam for NLU Delhi and other law colleges.

DU JAT (CUET)

Delhi University admission for BBA, BMS, BBE (now via CUET).

St. Xavier’s Entrance

For BMS, B.Com at St. Xavier’s Mumbai/Kolkata.
