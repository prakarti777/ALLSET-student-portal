Why Choose Vocational Courses?

Learn by Doing

Hands-on labs and projects build practical skills that employers value.

Faster to Job

Short durations and focused curricula help you start earning sooner.

Affordable Options

Choose from government-funded, NGO, or private institutes to match your budget.

Eligibility & Pathways

After 10th (Matric)

  • Certificate (3–12 months) in IT, Electrical, Beauty & Wellness, Retail, etc.
  • Diploma (1–2 years) in Lab Tech, Hotel Operations, Engineering trades.
  • Bridge to Class 12 (open schooling) while pursuing skills.

After 12th

  • Advanced Diplomas and Industry Certifications.
  • Apprenticeships/On-the-job Training with stipends.
  • Pathways to Degree (lateral entry) in some streams.

Typical Documents

  • Photo ID, 10th/12th mark sheet, transfer/TC (if required).
  • Passport photos, caste/income certificate (for scholarships).
  • Updated CV (for placements/internships).

Where to Study

  • Govt ITIs/Polytechnics, NSDC partners (PMKVY), Sector Skill Councils.
  • University skill centres, community colleges, private academies.
  • Apprenticeship portals & employer-led programs.

How to Apply

A simple step-by-step to get started.

  1. Pick your domain and shortlist 3–5 institutes near you.
  2. Check eligibility, duration, fees, and placement support.
  3. Collect documents and fill the application (online/offline).
  4. Attend counselling/aptitude tests if required.
  5. Apply for scholarships or fee waivers where eligible.
  6. Start classes and track progress via projects/internships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are vocational courses recognized for jobs?

Yes. Recruiters value practical skills and certified training. NSDC/SSC-aligned courses and apprenticeships add credibility.

Can I do these after 10th?

Absolutely. Many certificate and diploma programs are open after 10th. You can also continue Class 12 via open schooling in parallel.

What is the typical fee range?

From ₹8,000 for short certificates to ₹1,20,000 for advanced diplomas, depending on city, institute, and facilities.

Do institutes provide placements?

Many do. Check recent placement data, partner companies, and internship options before enrolling.
